# 🚀 Invest.ai

An AI-powered investment research platform that helps investors, analysts, and venture capitalists evaluate startups using artificial intelligence.

## 📖 Overview

Invest.ai simplifies investment research by automating startup analysis, market research, risk assessment, and report generation. Users can upload startup details or pitch decks and receive actionable insights powered by AI.

---

## ✨ Key Features

### 🤖 AI Startup Analysis
- Business model evaluation
- Startup health scoring
- SWOT analysis
- Risk assessment
- Growth potential prediction

### 📊 Financial Insights
- Revenue projections
- Burn rate analysis
- Investment readiness score
- Funding recommendations

### 📈 Market Research
- Industry trend analysis
- Competitor identification
- Market size estimation
- Opportunity detection

### 📄 AI Report Generation
Generate detailed reports including:
- Executive Summary
- Strengths & Weaknesses
- Opportunities & Threats
- Financial Insights
- Investment Recommendation

### 📁 Pitch Deck Analysis
- Upload PDF pitch decks
- Extract startup information
- Analyze business strategy
- Generate investment insights

### 🔍 Smart Search & Filtering
- Search startups
- Filter by industry
- Filter by funding stage
- Sort by AI score

### 📱 Responsive Design
- Mobile Friendly
- Tablet Optimized
- Desktop Ready
