# 📌 Project Overview

Invest.ai is an AI-powered platform developed to simplify startup investment research.

Traditionally, investors spend hours reading pitch decks, business plans, market reports, and financial statements before making an investment decision.

This platform automates that process using Generative AI.

Users simply upload startup information or enter startup details.

The AI then performs:

- Business Model Analysis
- SWOT Analysis
- Risk Detection
- Competitor Analysis
- Financial Prediction
- Market Opportunity Analysis
- Investment Recommendation

Finally, the system generates a complete investment report.

---

# 🎯 Problem Statement

Startup investment research is

- Time consuming
- Expensive
- Requires expert knowledge
- Difficult for beginner investors

Invest.ai solves these problems by generating AI-powered investment reports within seconds.

---

# 💡 Solution

The platform integrates modern web technologies with Google Gemini AI to provide intelligent startup analysis.

Instead of manually evaluating companies, users receive structured insights instantly.

# ⚙ Complete Working Flow

## Step 1

User visits the application.

↓

## Step 2

User signs in.

↓

## Step 3

User enters startup information

or

Uploads Pitch Deck PDF.

↓

## Step 4

Frontend validates data.

↓

## Step 5

Backend receives request.

↓

## Step 6

Backend prepares AI prompt.

↓

## Step 7

Prompt is sent to Gemini AI.

↓

## Step 8

Gemini analyzes

- Business
- Competition
- Revenue
- Risks
- Market
- Funding

↓

## Step 9

Gemini returns structured JSON response.

↓

## Step 10

Backend formats response.

↓

## Step 11

Frontend displays

- Charts
- Investment Score
- SWOT
- Recommendation
- Financial Analysis

↓

## Step 12

User can download or save the report.
